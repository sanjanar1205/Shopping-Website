ABOUT US


MyShop is a home to a multitude of leading international and national brands for women wears like tops, bottoms, ethnic and dresses. We aspire to provide our customers a memorable international shopping experience. We are one of the largest chain of department stores across India.

We are more than just clothing brand.

We aren't claiming to save the world from itself but we're committed to not making the same mistakes as our predecessors.

We are real. There's real technology driving our company. There are real people leading us. From the highly-experienced and educated, to the creative thinking, we have built MyShop with authenticity, talent, and vision.

We exist to support people in their pursuit of style, and we take pride in doing it through honesty premium quality, and tech-powered betterment.

We're not looking for a bright future, we're here to make the future bright

Our Vision Is, “To be an inspirational and trusted brand, transforming customers' lives through fashion and delightful shopping experience every time.” We have a team of professional associates who strive endlessly to provide the best shopping experience to each of our customers. We have adopted a new philosophy of "Start Something New" to give retail a new dimension and innovation is our key driver to attain excellence in customer service.
